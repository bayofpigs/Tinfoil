*FINTOIL*

an onion routing service for secure web browsing

...

OVERVIEW: Want to browse the web, but believe that even Tor poses too high of a risk due to low latency? Fintoil will securely retrieve the html page for you via onion routing.

USAGE: email Fintoil with the body containing the RSA encrypted details of the content you want to retrieve. Fintoil will email you back with the html page encrypted with your key.

1. Create a textfile containing your email, your desired URL, latency value, and public key.

$ vim oldFile.txt

~ email: user@email.com

~ site: www.url.com

~ delay: latency

~ key: public key

2. Encrypt the textfile with your public key.

$ gpg --encrypt --batch -r 5D8315B9 --output newFile.txt oldFile.txt

You can download our public key here.

3. Send us your encrypted file!
